# Spaced repetition and creativity

[[Some kinds of creative insight depend on long-term memory]]

## Baader-Meinhof effect and SRS

Gwern suggests that one role for SRS is to stimulate the Baader-Meinhof effect.

Also references [[Diaconis & Mosteller 1989]], which suggests a statistical explanation for apparent coincidences; an argument like “regression to the mean” can make newly-exposed ideas suddenly appear to be everywhere.
