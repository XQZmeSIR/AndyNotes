# Unity

Q. How to zoom the Scene view? A. Opt+Ctrl+Drag / Opt+RightClick+Drag; Scroll

Q. How to orbit/rotate the Scene view? A. Opt+Drag

Q. How to focus the Scene view on the selected object? A. F (with cursor over the Scene view)

Q. How to use Flythrough mode to navigate in Scene view? A. With right-click held, WASD / mouse-look

Q. How to move up/down in Flythrough mode? A. E/Q

Q. What keys switch between the six main tools? A. QWERTY
