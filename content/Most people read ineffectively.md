# Most people read ineffectively

  * [[Knowledge work should accrete]], but most reading does not
    * [[People seem to forget most of what they read, and they mostly don’t notice]]
    * [[Evergreen note-writing helps reading efforts accumulate]], but most people don’t do this
    * Of those that do, [[Note-writing practices are generally ineffective]]
    * However! [[The most effective readers and thinkers I know don’t take notes when reading]]
  * [[Skillful reading is often non-linear]], but most people read linearly
