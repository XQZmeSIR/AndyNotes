# AlterEgo

Surface electromyography-based [[Silent speech interface]] project by [[Arnav Kapur]], in [[Pattie Maes]]’s group at [[MIT Media Lab]]. First publication in 2018. Its key innovation (AFAICT) is to bring contemporary deep learning practices to the EMG signal processing.

  * A. Kapur, S. Kapur, and P. Maes, “AlterEgo: A Personalized Wearable Silent Speech Interface.” 23rd International Conference on Intelligent User Interfaces (IUI 2018), pp 43-53, March 5, 2018.
    * “On a standard digit recognition test, our system achieves a median accuracy of 92%” Very limited vocabularies (20 words).
  * Kapur, A., Sarawgi, U., Wadkins, E., Wu, M., Hollenstein, N., & Maes, P. (n.d.). Non-Invasive Silent Speech Recognition in Multiple Sclerosis with Dysphonia.
    * Expands to recognizing sentences from a limited set (15 phrases); achieves ~200 bits per minute at a mean accuracy of ~0.8
