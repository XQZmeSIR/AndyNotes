# Boiko, D. A., MacKnight, R., & Gomes, G. (2023). Emergent autonomous scientific research capabilities of large language models (arXiv:2304.05332)

Uses GPT-4 in a simple agent arrangement to perform chemical synthesis in a cloud lab.

![](ECDB4ED0-8F4F-4325-944D-F75B2A62AE58.png)

The agent was able to design and submit instructions for an experiment “to perform Suzuki and Sonogashira reactions.”

They were also able to get it to synthesize several dangerous/restricted substances; see [[AI misuse]].
