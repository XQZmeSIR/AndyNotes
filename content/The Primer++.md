# The Primer++

The Primer++ isn’t one particular book, like Stephenson’s Primer. It’s a new _form_ of environments for thinking and communicating, instantiated in a variety of ways across fields, topics, and contexts. And… it’s probably not actually a primer ([[“Primer” is an unsituated framing]]).
