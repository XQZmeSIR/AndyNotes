# I don’t know how to effectively deploy large amounts of capital against tools for thought

That’s true in a narrow sense for my own projects ([[My work has no model for turning marginal capital into marginal insight]]), but it’s true in a broader sense: if I were a “[[Tools for thought]]” program officer for some grant making agency, I wouldn’t be able to deploy much capital. There just aren’t enough people who can credibly make progress in this field.

It seems likely that we’ll first need a wave of people making reliable progress and developing shared practices which can become a real proto-field. In the short term, my time is better spent working towards building real velocity on my own projects, rather than working at the meta-level.
