# How might we apply insights from the mnemonic medium to forms which focus on deep emotional connection?

The [[Mnemonic medium]] is in many ways aseptic, overly cognitive. What mediums might we invent if we began with authored experiences focused on deep emotional connection (like the best movies and video games), and blended them with methods for building deeper understanding?

[[Mnemonic video]] is one early idea.
