# OpenStax

Non-profit out of Rice University creating permissively-licensed online textbooks. They started by covering the 25 most popular undergraduate courses; now they’ve expanded to many other topics.

[[OpenStax]]

Recommended to me initially by [[Kumar Garg]].

Hm… it seems they’re doing experiments on [[Retrieval practice]]! They’ve developed something called “[[OpenStax Tutor]]”, with support in 10 of their books.

And:

> Automatic question generation > Our goal is to extract the base knowledge components from OpenStax textbooks > and construct meaningful questions that can be used for practice by > students. We’ve been able to generate simple recall questions across various > formats including simple multiple choice, true/false, and choose-all-that- > apply questions.

Wow: we sound quite aligned, actually, except perhaps for the educationalism.
