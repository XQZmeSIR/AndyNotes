# AI misinformation risk

I’m mostly not terribly worried about “spambots flooding social media”-type [[AI risk]] concerns. But collecting instances which seem more worrisome.

  * <https://nypost.com/2023/04/12/ai-clones-teen-girls-voice-in-1m-kidnapping-scam/> a scammer (purportedly) generated audio of a woman’s sobbing daughter asking for help, demanded a ransom
  * <https://twitter.com/browserdotsys/status/1643681089762861066> a bunch of wild ideas…
    * “herobot- a chatbot RLHF’d to provide the most persuasive personalized arguments for suicide and then finds teen girls to talk to online” (sort of [[AI misuse]], I guess)
    * "defamationbot- starts by doing web searches to gather true information about someone and then modifies it to create the most plausible damaging lies”
    * “sailerbot- a bot trained on someone’s writing that posts racist comments under their name on random blogs, news comment sections, etc”
