# What might constitute canonical uses of the mnemonic medium?

One important category is concentrated sources of platform knowledge which many people struggle to acquire ([[The mnemonic medium is particularly valuable for platform knowledge]]). For example:

  * [[Molecular Biology of the Cell]]
  * Kandel’s _Principles of Neural Science_
  * [[ The Feynman Lectures on Physics]]

Outside of platform knowledge, our understanding of the applications of the mnemonic medium is much weaker ([[How might the mnemonic medium enable readers in genres outside platform knowledge?]]).

A canonical use must also be authored virtuosically, not just in the prose but also in the prompts. See [[Important attributes of good spaced repetition memory prompts]]
