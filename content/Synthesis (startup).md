# Synthesis (startup)

[[Synthesis]]

A venture-backed educational technology startup which has shipped [[collaborative strategy games]] emphasizing planning and leadership; now appears mostly focused on [[Synthesis Tutor]].

Trades heavily on its cofounder, Joshua Dahn, who started an unusual project- based learning school called Ad Astra at the behest of Elon Musk. From what I can tell, this isn’t Josh’s full-time focus, and the other cofounder, Chrisman Frank (formerly a generalist product person at Classdojo), is driving in practice. Very marketing/buzz-heavy.
