# What is the causal impact of the mnemonic medium’s review sessions on reader retention?

[[Quantum Country readers reliably develop detailed retention for embedded questions]], but that data is correlative. What’s the causal impact of a marginal session, at a particular time?

We’ve run a few studies on this question; see [[Quantum Country efficacy RCTs]] for more.
