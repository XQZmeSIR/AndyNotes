# Audiobooks are produced under a wide variety of business models

[[Audiobooks]] are made in a surprising variety of organizational contexts.

  *  **Publishers** produce audiobooks for the market alongside print releases of books they publish
  *  **Distributors** like Audible arrange to produce audiobooks of already-published print books
    * They also commission original, audiobook-native works.
  *  **Authors** commission or self-produce audiobook versions of their books
  *  **Freelancers** produce audiobooks in response to commissions or bounties on markets like [[ACX]]
  *  **Volunteers** collectively produce open-license audiobooks
