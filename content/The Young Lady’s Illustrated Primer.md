# The Young Lady’s Illustrated Primer

As described in _The Diamond Age_ , the Young Lady’s Illustrated Primer (often just called “The Primer”) is a fictional electronic book which facilitates the remote, asynchronous education of children. Its curriculum includes practical skills like math and reading, principally via [[Inquiry-based learning]], but [[The Primer’s goal is to produce creative, subversive youth]].

For many in educational technology, the Primer represents an inspiring vision for a future of education:

  * [[The Primer reminds people of their own experiences with profound enablement]]
  * [[The Primer doesn’t appear to coerce its students]]
  * [[The Primer is scalable]]

Unfortunately, I don’t think it’s a viable vision: [[The Primer isn’t a viable enabling environment]].
