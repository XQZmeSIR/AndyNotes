# Does concept mapping involve implicit retrieval practice?

Naively, I would expect this activity to evoke [[Implicit retrieval practice]], but [[Karpicke, J. D., & Blunt, J. R. (2011). Retrieval Practice Produces More Learning than Elaborative Studying with Concept Mapping. Science, 331(6018), 772–775]] find otherwise, and so does Kornell (2012) (as cited in [[Agarwal, P. K., Nunes, L. D., & Blunt, J. R. (2021). Retrieval Practice Consistently Benefits Student Learning: A Systematic Review of Applied Research in Schools and Classrooms. Educational Psychology Review.]]).
