# O’Reilly, T., Sinclair, G. P., & McNamara, D. S. (2004). Reading Strategy Training: Automated Verses Live. Proceedings of the Annual Meeting of the Cognitive Science Society, 26.

Demonstrates that [[iSTART]], a system which attempts to automate [[Self-explanation]] strategies training, performs about as well as the training delivered by humans in [[McNamara, D. (2004). SERT: Self-explanation reading training. Discourse Processes, 38(1), 1–30]]. In this experiment, there’s moderate improvement, but only for text-based questions.

Students are given a knowledge test, as in (McNamara, 2004), but no extended analysis here about high-vs-low-knowledge students, other than a note that high-knowledge students performed better. ![](image.png)
