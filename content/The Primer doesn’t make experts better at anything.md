# The Primer doesn’t make experts better at anything

In _The Diamond Age,_ the Primer contains a nano fabrication facility, a telescope, a microscope, and other countless wonders—but none of these is superior to those an expert practitioner would already possess. The Primer isn’t an [[Enabling environment]] for experts. It only aspires to be one for children, not by “raising the ceiling” on what anyone can do but by “lowering the floor.”

This is a limited approach: [[Powerful enabling environments focus on expert use]].
