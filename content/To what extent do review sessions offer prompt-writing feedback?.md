# To what extent do review sessions offer prompt-writing feedback

[[Writing good spaced repetition memory prompts is hard]], but to make matters worse, it’s hard for many novices to actually _notice_ that writing good prompts is hard. There aren’t any strong feedback mechanisms.

With understanding of what makes a good (or bad) prompt, review sessions could offer some feedback to an attentive and reflective reviewer—though it won’t necessarily provide the knowledge needed to correct those prompts.
