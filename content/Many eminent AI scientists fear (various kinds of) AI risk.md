# Many eminent AI scientists fear (various kinds of) AI risk

[[AI risk]] is not a niche fear; it’s fairly widespread among serious practitioners.

  * See e.g. signatories of the [[Slow down AI]] FHI letter.
  * Yoshua Bengio (2023-04-05) [[calls]] the acceleration “unexpected”: “It is because there is an unexpected acceleration –I probably would not have signed such a letter a year ago– that we need to take a step back, and that my opinion on these topics has changed.”
  * April 2023; Hinton quits Google so that he can speak openly about the risks
  * Paul Christiano [[calls]]: “I think [superintelligent AI]() is the single most likely reason that I will personally die.”
  * Douglas Hofstadter [[says]]: “I think it’s terrifying. I hate it. I think about it practically all the time, every single day. [Q: Wow.]() And it overwhelms me and depresses me in a way that I haven’t been depressed for a very long time.”
