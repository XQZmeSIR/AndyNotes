# Hermann Ebbinghaus

Discovered the [[Spacing effect]], one of the founders of experimental psychology and the methodology which preceded [[Cognitive psychology]]; see [[Ebbinghaus, H. (1913). Memory: A Contribution to Experimental Psychology (H. A. Ruger & C. E. Bussenius, Trans.). (Original work published 1885)]] for more.

Ebbinghaus’s experiments were important to psychology because they demonstrated that {mental phenomena could be reliably quantified and studied}.
