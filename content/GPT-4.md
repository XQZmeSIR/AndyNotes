# GPT-4

[[Large language models]] from [[OpenAI]], released March 2023.

As far as I can tell from the paper, it’s a (weak, amnesiac) AGI: its performance on basically any short-lived intellectual task can match or exceed a typical well-educated teen. Astonishing.

Q. GPT-2 FLOPs? A. $1.5 \times 10^21$ ([[source]])

Q. GPT-3 FLOPs? A. $3.1 \times 10^23$ ([[source]])

Q. GPT-4 FLOPs? A. $2.1 \times 10^25$ ([[source]])

Q. A100 FLOPs? A. $3.1 \times 10^14$ (312 TFLOPs; FP32)

Q. H100 FLOPs? A. $9.9 \times 10^14$ (989 TFLOPs; FP32; SXM)

Q. M1 Max FLOPs? A. $10^13$ (10.4 TFLOPs; FP32)

Q. Nvidia RTX 4090 FLOPs? A. $8.3 \times 10^13$ (82.6 TFLOPS; FP32)
