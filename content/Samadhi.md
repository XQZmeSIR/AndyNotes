# Samadhi

In the Theravada tradition, _samadhi_ refers to continuous attention on a specific [[Meditation]] object.

This kind of attention is challenging to cultivate. Per [[The Mind Illuminated - Culadasa]]: “the untrained mind produces {distractions} that lead to {forgetting}, which results in {mind-wandering}.”
