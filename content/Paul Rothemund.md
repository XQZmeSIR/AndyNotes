# Paul Rothemund

Pioneer of DNA origami and nano-scale biomolecular engineering techniques.

  * Early career on DNA computers:
    * PhD in computer science, 2001 (USC), on self-assembling DNA computers: XOR gates, etc.
    * 2002 paper solving 3-SAT (N=20) on a DNA computer
  * Key 2006 paper on self-folding DNA for general patterns and shapes
    * [[Folding DNA to create nanoscale shapes and patterns]]
