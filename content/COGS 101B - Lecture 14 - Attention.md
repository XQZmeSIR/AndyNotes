# COGS 101B - Lecture 14 - Attention

{Stroop interference}: it’s harder to name the colors of printed words when those words are {color names}.

Attention may explain Stroop interference: we read words automatically, and executive control is needed to override that process. People with disorders impairing executive control see more diminished effects.
