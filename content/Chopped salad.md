# Chopped salad

General formula: greens, vegetable, pulses/beans/grains, fruit, nuts, cheese, pickles/olives; typically vinaigrette.

## Seasonal salad produce faves

  * Spring: baby greens; [[Snap peas]], English [[Peas]] (blanched or mi cuit), [[Asparagus]] (raw), [[Fennel]], [[Radish]]; [[Cherries]], strawberries
  * Early summer: little gems; [[Corn]], roasted [[Summer squash]], roasted [[Broccolini]], cauliflower, [[Cucumber]]; stone fruit
  * Late summer: little gems; peppers, [[Corn]], [[Shelling beans]], [[String beans]], [[Tomato]]; melon
  * Fall: [[Kale]]; peppers, roasted [[Brussels sprouts]], roasted [[Beet]], [[Swiss chard]] stems; pear, apple, pomegranate
  * Winter: chicories; confited[[Parsnip]] / [[Celery root]] / [[Turnip]], roasted [[Winter squash]]; citrus, apple
