# E-reading popularity sharply trails all book-reading

In [[a 2018 Pew survey]], the plurality of respondents read print books only. About 3/4 of respondents read any book in the past year; only 1/4 read an e-book in the past year. The trend is flat-to- downwards. But audiobooks are on the rise.

Possible cause: [[Reading texts on computers is unpleasant]].
