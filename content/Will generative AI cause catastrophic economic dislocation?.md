# Will generative AI cause catastrophic economic dislocation?

One facet of [[AI risk]].

  * <https://economics.mit.edu/sites/default/files/2024-04/The%20Simple%20Macroeconomics%20of%20AI.pdf￼>

    * Acemoglu finds much more modest impacts over the next 10 years than Briggs or Felten, predicts slight wage growth for low-income workers.
  * [[Briggs, J., Kodnani, D., Hatzius, J., & Pierdomenico, G. (2023). Large Effects of Artificial Intelligence on Economic Growth. Goldman Sachs.]]

    * Perhaps 7% of US jobs will be mostly replaced by generative AI. Historic comparisons suggest that many (but not all) of these people will be re-employed elsewhere. Estimated 7% GDP increase over ten years.
  * [[Felten et al. Occupational Heterogeneity in Exposure to Generative AI (2023)]]

    * O*NET-based modeling; finds AI will disproportionately impact highly educated, highly paid, highly creative occupations
  * Manning, S., Mishkin, P., Hadfield, G., & Eisner, E. (2023). _A Research Agenda for Assessing the Economic Impacts of Code Generation Models_.

    * OpenAI is looking to evaluate the economic impacts of Codex as a possible input to deployment criteria.
