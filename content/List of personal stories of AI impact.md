# List of personal stories of AI impact

The softer side of [[AI risk]]. We can think about societal-scale impact ([[Will generative AI cause catastrophic economic dislocation?]]), but it’s important to stay grounded in the individual.

Do you have a good story for my list? [[Please share]]!

## Job displacement

  * [[I lost everything that made me love my job through Midjourney over night.]] Redditor: “I am not an artist anymore… all I do is prompting… the reason I went to be a 3D artist in the first place is gone.”
  * “[[It happened to me today]]” - Redditor: "I literally lost my biggest and best client to ChatGPT today. This client is my main source of income, he’s a marketer who outsources the majority of his copy and content writing to me. … I just signed up for Doordash as a driver.”
  * “[[I wish GPT4 had never happened]]” - a friend in India has a job as an annotator of biotech papers; author was able to reproduce their work in a fraction of the time with GPT-4; fears they will lose their job. ([[via]] [[Michael Nielsen]]
  * “[[proceeded to make me an offer that was a full $25,000 less than what I asked for the position]]” - 3D animator discussing a negotiation for a project
