# Timeful text

[[Mass mediums mostly lack an authored time dimension beyond a day]]. This seems like a powerful authorial dimension to explore, as illustrated by e.g. [[Guided meditation apps are naturally spread over time]].

We might call texts with an authored time dimension _timeful texts._

  * [[ The mnemonic medium keeps readers in contact with material over time]]
  * [[The mnemonic medium can be adapted to author an experience which unfolds over time]]
    * [[Mnemonic texts could be serialized in small sections over time, timed to reader recall]] (like [[Execute Program’s lessons don’t unlock until you’ve successfully reviewed their prerequisites]])

Some examples:

  * [[The Artist’s Way - Julia Cameron]]
  * [[Guided meditation app]]
