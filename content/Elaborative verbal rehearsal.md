# Elaborative verbal rehearsal

A reading comprehension strategy in which readers summarize a text and elaborate it by synthesizing, giving examples, and drawing personal connections. Effectively a highly specialized type of [[Self- explanation]]. The literature using this term seems to mostly dead-end in preference to Chi’s term, which also encompasses a wider range of behaviors.

Presumably this operationalizes some combination of:

  * [[Retrieval practice]]
  * [[Elaborative encoding]]
  * [[Generation effect]]
  * [[Self-explanation effect]]
