# Eckhart Tolle’s pause symbol

In _The Power of Now_ , Eckhart Tolle uses a special symbol which indicates “that you may want to stop reading or a moment, become still, and feel and experience the truth of what has just been said.”

An interesting counterpoint to [[Most explanatory media place heavy metacognitive demands on participants]].
